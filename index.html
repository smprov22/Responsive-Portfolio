About Me

My name is Stephanie Provence and I'm taking this coding bootcamp to learn new skills and hopefully enter a new career path. Although I expect this to be very challenging, I think the reward of a new, fulfilling career will be well worth it in the long run.

I am originally from Minnesota and moved to Sacramento with my family in 1999. I went to High School here and then, after a brief stint in Seattle to play college soccer, I went to CSU Monterey Bay for college.

Upon graduation, I moved to Los Angeles to pursue a career in editing in the TV/Film industry. Although I did ok down there, I never made it as far as I wanted, and after having my daughter in 2017, my husband and I decided that living near family while my daughter is young is more important than working in the film industry.
